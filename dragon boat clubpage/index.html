One boat, One family - SJSU Dragon Boat

What is dragon boat?

"For those unfamiliar with the sport, dragon boating simply put, is a boat of 20 paddlers, a drummer and a steers person paddling to cross the finish faster than their competition. It's a team sport in its purest form that encompasses the elements of power, speed, synchronization and endurance." - CDBA

Who are we?

Straight Outta Silicon Valley - Representing San Jose State University, San Jose Dragon Boat (SJDB) is a team of dedicated individuals, striving to become the premier dragon boat team in the South Bay. Stay tuned for more information on us, club activities, and race updates!
