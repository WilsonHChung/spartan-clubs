Club Sport: Badminton

A Club Sport is a group of students who meet regularly to pursue an interest in a competitive collegiate activity at the state, regional, and national level.

Who are we?

Badminton is an individual sport. However, on SJSU Badminton Team, we strived together to improve our skills and have fun in competing. We provide moral support and feedback to our teammates during tournaments and practices. We practice on a weekly basis to improve ourself and help others. SJSU Badminton Team is here to promote sportsmanship and results of hard work.
